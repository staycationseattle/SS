PARKS

Olympic Sculpture Park

Olympic Square park is the perfect place for an

artistic afternoon in Seattle.The park was designed

by the Seattle Art Museum so it contains beautiful

architecture and greenery. It's a great place for

a picnic with your loved ones.

Address: 2901 Western Ave, Seattle, WA 98121

When to visit: on a warm sunny day

Lake Union Park

Lake Union Park is perfect for a sunny

afternoon by the water.Visitors can enjoy a

pedestrian bridge and a playful fountain.

Suitable for children, adults and

people looking to have fun.

Address: 860 Terry Ave N, Seattle, WA 98109

When to visit: on a warm clear day

Cal Anderson Park

The Cal Anderson Park is a large welcoming park

in a quiet clean neighborhood in Seattle.

Perfect if you are looking to switch it up

and visit a more funky and architectural park.

It is enjoyable for the whole family!

Address: 1635 11th Ave, Seattle, WA 98122

When to visit: Sunny spring and summer days.

Kubota Garden

The Kubota Garden is an enchanting japanese

garden located in south seattle.Come here to take

instagram worthy snaps!This garden is filled with a

variety of beautiful flowers and plants; many

Seattleites take their wedding photos here.

Address: 9817 55th Ave S, Seattle, WA 98118

When to visit: On a clear days.

Seward Park

Seward Park is the place to be at if you are an

adventurous person but do not want to leave the

comfort of the city. It is a great place to hike

around or go on a bike ride. The park is located

by lake Washington so you can also enjoy the

tranquility of the like when you visit.

Address:5900 Lake Washington Blvd S,

Seattle, WA 98118

When to visit: Any time of the year
