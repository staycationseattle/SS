I-90 TrailS

The I-90 trail is a beautiful breathtaking trail along a busy

freeway. The trail starts in Seattle and spans 10 miles east

across Lake Washington, Mercer Island and ends in Bellevue.

The I-90 trail goes across a floating bridge with a great

panoramic view of Downtown Seattle and lake Washington.

It is best to enjoy the trail on any type of day by biking

but walking works too!

Endpoints: 1007 12th Ave. S, Factoria Blvd SE & SE 36th St

When to visit: Anytime! For the best views it is best to

go on a clear day.

Artists at Play

Artists at play is a creative, fun, and interactive playground

for people of all ages. This playground is filled with funky

shaped slides and climbing structures; it is the coolest

playground ever! Many visiting families wish that they had this

in their hometown so we are lucky to have it in Seattle.

Address: 305 Harrison St, Seattle, WA 98109

When to visit:To avoid the crowds it is best to go on weekdays

and on non-rainy days.
