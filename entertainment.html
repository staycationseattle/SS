Pacific Science Center

The Pacific Science Center is perfect for a day trip with

the family. Children of all ages are sure to be engaged by

the various exhibitions and activities. Even if science is

not your cup of tea you are guaranteed to enjoy your visit.

Address: 200 Second Ave. North, Seattle, WA 98109

When to visit: Anytime!

Seattle Art Museum (SAM)

The Seattle Art Museum (SAM) is known for its wide range

of art and culture. The experience of going to SAM is

valuable for art viewers of any level and specialty.

Everyone is sure to be entranced by the Seattle Art Museum!

Address: 1300 1st Ave, Seattle, WA 98101

When to visit: Anytime

Seattle Great Wheel

The Seattle Great Wheel is a massive ferris wheel

by the port of Seattle. Riders get a bird's eye

view of the city. It is best to go on a clear day

to get the best view. The Seattle Great Wheel is an

enjoyable ride for people of all ages!

Address: 1301 Alaskan Way, Seattle, WA 98101

When to visit: On any clear day

Chihuly Garden and Glass

The Chihuly Garden and Glass is an exhibition in

Downtown Seattle featuring the glass works of Dale

Chihuly. Many tourists review this place as a must

visit for all.The exhibition is very beautiful,

intricate and impressive.

Address: 305 Harrison St, Seattle, WA 98109

When to visit: Any clear day.

Museum of Pop Culture (MoPoP)

The Museum of Pop Culture (MoPoP) is an astonishing

welly designed museum of contemporary pop culture. The

building itself is jaw dropping with vibrant colors

you can only expect nothing but the best inside. MoPoP

features pop culture artifacts from various artists

throughout history.

Address: 325 5th Ave N, Seattle, WA 98109

When to visit: Any day
